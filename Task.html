Recipe 1: Classic Spaghetti Bolognese

Ingredients:

  1. 1. Tbsp oil
  2. 2. Large onion, Finely chopped
  3. 3. 500g lean beef mince
  4. 4. 1/4 Cup tomato paste
  5. 5. 400g can crushed tomatoes, in juice
  6. 6. 1 tsp basil
  7. 7. 1 tsp oregano
  8. 8. 3 Cup water
  9. 9. Salt
  10. 10. Black pepper
  11. 11. 250g Spaghetti, Cooked
  12. 12. Puhoi valley Parmesan Cheese, Grated

Instructions:

  1. 1. Heat oil a large frying pan. Add onion and cook until golden, stirring constantly.
  2. 2. Stir in meat and quickly brown. Add tomato paste and conned tomatoes.
  3. 3. Stir in basil, oregano and water.
  4. 4. Bring to the boil,n reduce heat and cook uncovered for 45 minutes or until mixture is a thick souce consistency.
  5. 5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. 6. Serve over hot pasta garnished with parmesan cheese.

Recipe 2: Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients:

  1. 1. 125g Butter, Softened
  2. 2. 1 tsp Vanilla essence
  3. 3. 3/4 Cup Chelsea Caster Sugar
  4. 4. 1 Egg yolk
  5. 5. 1 1/2 Cups Edmonds Gluten Free Self Raising Flour
  6. 6. 2 tsp Water
  7. 7. 1/2 Cup dark Chocolate Chips

Instructions:

  1. 1. Preheat the oven to 180°C conventional or 160°C fan forced.
  2. 2. Grease or line two baking tray with baking paper.
  3. 3. Beat the butter, vanilla essence, sugar and egg yolk in a small bowl with an electric mixer on low speed for 4 minutes until smooth.
  4. 4. Add in sifted flour, water and chocolate chips, then mix with a wooden spoon to make a soft dough.
  5. 5. Roll teaspoonfuls of mixture into balls or divide dough into required numbers of biscuits.
  6. 6. Place balls approx, 5cm apart on prepared trays.
  7. 7. Bake for 15 minutes or until lightly browned.
  8. 8. Leave on the tray for 5 minutes than place on a wire rack to cool.
